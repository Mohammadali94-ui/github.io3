A Mechanical Masterpiece

Released in 1900, the Underwood No. 5 quickly became the world’s most popular typewriter. It introduced true “visible writing” and a robust mechanical system that defined the standard for decades.

  • Durable cast-iron frame
  • 250+ precision moving parts
  • Visible typing — a revolutionary invention
  • Trusted by reporters, authors, and businesses worldwide

How Can We Help?

For ribbon replacement, mechanical repairs, or cleaning, please refer to the following:

  • Replace ink ribbon every 3–4 weeks
  • Oil mechanical joints monthly
  • Avoid dust buildup with a cloth cover
  • Use original Underwood metal spools

Need help? Call us at: 1-800-UNDERWOOD
